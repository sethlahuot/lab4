Welcome Kampong Speu

Kampong Speu is a province located in southwestern Cambodia. Its name means "Port of Starfruit," with "Kampong" meaning "port" or "harbor," and "Speu" referring to "starfruit." The province is known for its diverse landscapes, including mountains, plains, and rivers.
The provincial capital is Chbar Mon. Kampong Speu is situated near the Cardamom Mountains and Phnom Aural, the highest peak in Cambodia. The province is primarily agricultural, with rice farming and sugar production being important economic activities.
