Welcome Kampot

Kampot is a province located in southern Cambodia, along the Gulf of Thailand. Its name refers to the "Port of Pot," with "Kampot" possibly derived from "Kampong" (port) and "Pot," though the exact origin is debated. The province is known for its scenic coastal landscapes, rivers, and mountains.
The provincial capital is also named Kampot. The province is famous for its agricultural products, particularly Kampot pepper, which has gained international recognition. Additionally, the Bokor Mountain and National Park, with its cool climate and historic French colonial buildings, is a major attraction in the area.
