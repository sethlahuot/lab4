Welcome Kampong Chhnang

Kampong Chhnang is a province located in central Cambodia, along the Tonlé Sap River. Its name means "Port of Pottery" in Khmer, with "Kampong" meaning "port" or "harbor," and "Chhnang" meaning "pot." The province is historically significant for its pottery-making traditions, reflecting Cambodia's cultural heritage.
The capital of Kampong Chhnang is also called Kampong Chhnang, and the province is known for its agricultural activities, especially rice farming and fishing, due to its proximity to the Tonlé Sap Lake and River. Additionally, the province's scenic landscapes and floating villages contribute to its cultural richness.
