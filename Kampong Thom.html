Welcome Kampong Thom

Kampong Thom is a province located in central Cambodia. Its name means "Great Port" or "Large Port," with "Kampong" meaning "port" or "harbor" and "Thom" meaning "big" or "great." The province is known for its historical significance, being home to several ancient temples and archaeological sites.
The capital of Kampong Thom is also called Kampong Thom. The province is situated along the Stung Sen River and serves as a key agricultural area, with rice farming being a major industry. Its proximity to the famous Sambor Prei Kuk temple complex adds to its cultural importance.
